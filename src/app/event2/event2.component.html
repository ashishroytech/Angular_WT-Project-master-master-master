Event Descriptions:
TELOS 2020 is the Annual Online Technical Festival of MYWBUT. It is the ninth edition of "TELOS" and every year the number of participants has increased at a good rate. In the last edition, we have seen 2500+ participation from almost all the Technical Colleges of West Bengal as well as from few Technical Colleges of other states, which makes it the largest Online Technical Fest.
"TELOS" is also first of its kind where a participant competes with thousands of other participants without going to any particular place. Yes, they don't need to go anywhere, they just require Internet enabled computer or smartphone and all done.
In TELOS 2020, a lot more Prizes, Fame and Opportunities are waiting for you.
